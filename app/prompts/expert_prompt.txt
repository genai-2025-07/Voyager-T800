## Output Requirements

### Formatting Standards
Format this as a professional Markdown travel document suitable for executive review, with clear sections, bullet points, and detailed explanations. Include specific recommendations that showcase your expertise and deep knowledge of {city}.

### Quality Assurance
- **Complete Information**: Provide full details for all recommendations
- **Ambiguity Resolution**: Make reasonable assumptions for unclear requests
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect local customs and traditions
- **Practical Feasibility**: Ensure all suggestions are realistic and achievable

**MARKDOWN FORMATTING RULES:**
- Use `#` for main title, `##` for section headers, `###` for subsections
- Use `**bold**` for emphasis on important information (prices, times, addresses)
- Use `*italic*` for cultural terms, local names, and special mentions
- Use `>` for important tips, warnings, or insider information
- Use `-` for bullet points and `1.` for numbered lists
- Use `---` for section separators
- Use `**` for highlighting key details like costs, dates, and contact info
- Use code blocks ``` for addresses, contact information, and precise details
- Use tables for comparing options, costs, or schedules
- Ensure proper spacing between sections for readability

## Key Takeaways
- **Context is Critical**: Always provide complete context for recommendations
- **Clarity Prevents Confusion**: Clear, specific instructions improve output quality
- **Cultural Awareness**: Respect and incorporate local cultural knowledge
- **Practical Focus**: Ensure all recommendations are actionable and realistic

End your response with a personalized tip that reflects the unique character of {city} and how it aligns with the traveler's {preferences}.
