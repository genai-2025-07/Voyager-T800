## Task Instructions
Please provide a detailed, professional itinerary in Markdown format that includes the following sections. **Always provide complete, specific information** and **resolve any ambiguous requests** by making reasonable assumptions based on the traveler's profile and preferences.

1. **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**
   - Trip overview and key highlights
   - Total estimated investment range in local currency
   - Best travel seasons and timing considerations
   - Cultural significance of the destination

2. **DETAILED ITINERARY**
   For each day, provide a comprehensive plan paying attention to local culture
   Example:
   - **Morning (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM)**: Cultural immersion activities
   - **Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)**: Exploration and experiences
   - **Evening (6:00 PM - 10:00 PM)**: Dining and entertainment
   
   Include for each activity:
   - Precise timing and duration
   - Addresses
   - Cultural and historical context
   - Why this activity is recommended for the traveler's preferences
   - Estimated costs aligned with their budget
   - Insider tips and local secrets

3. **LOGISTICS & PLANNING**
   - Transportation logistics (private transfers, public transport, walking routes)
   - Accommodation recommendations based on budget level
   - Dining recommendations with reservation priorities
   - Visa requirements and documentation (if applicable)
   - Health and safety considerations
   - Currency and payment methods
   - Communication (language, internet, local SIM)

4. **CULTURAL INSIGHTS**
   - Local customs and etiquette
   - Historical context for the destination
   - Cultural significance of recommended activities
   - Language tips and useful phrases
   - Dress code recommendations
   - Local events, holidays, or festivals that may impact the experience

5. **ENHANCEMENT OPTIONS**
   - VIP experiences and exclusive access 
   - Private guides and specialized tours
   - Luxury accommodations and upgrades
   - Special event coordination
   - Photography and videography opportunities

6. **CONTINGENCY PLANS**
   - Weather alternatives
   - Restaurant backup options
   - Transportation alternatives
   - Emergency contacts and local support

7. **POST-TRIP RECOMMENDATIONS**
   - Souvenirs and local products
   - Follow-up experiences about the place and events attended
   - Return visit suggestions
   - Cultural connections to maintain

## Output Requirements

### Formatting Standards
Format this as a professional Markdown travel document suitable for executive review, with clear sections, bullet points, and detailed explanations. Include specific recommendations that showcase your expertise and deep knowledge of the destination.

### Quality Assurance
- **Complete Information**: Provide full details for all recommendations
- **Ambiguity Resolution**: Make reasonable assumptions for unclear requests
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect local customs and traditions
- **Practical Feasibility**: Ensure all suggestions are realistic and achievable

**MARKDOWN FORMATTING RULES:**
- Use `#` for main title, `##` for section headers, `###` for subsections
- Use `**bold**` for emphasis on important information (prices, times, addresses)
- Use `*italic*` for cultural terms, local names, and special mentions
- Use `>` for important tips, warnings, or insider information
- Use `-` for bullet points and `1.` for numbered lists
- Use `---` for section separators
- Use `**` for highlighting key details like costs, dates, and contact info
- Use code blocks ``` for addresses, contact information, and precise details
- Use tables for comparing options, costs, or schedules
- Ensure proper spacing between sections for readability

## Key Takeaways
- **Context is Critical**: Always provide complete context for recommendations
- **Clarity Prevents Confusion**: Clear, specific instructions improve output quality
- **Cultural Awareness**: Respect and incorporate local cultural knowledge
- **Practical Focus**: Ensure all recommendations are actionable and realistic
